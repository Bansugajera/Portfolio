About

I'm a B.Tech student in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Pandit Deendayal Energy University, Gandhinagar, pursuing a Minor in Robotics. My work lives at the crossroads of photonics, signal intelligence, and autonomous systems.

My research focuses on Visible Light Communication (VLC) enhanced by Dynamic Intelligent Surfaces — a domain with profound implications for next-generation autonomous vehicle coordination and smart infrastructure.

I enjoy translating complex theoretical frameworks into working simulations using MATLAB, and building embedded systems that bring ideas to physical life. Whether it's mitigating chromatic dispersion in coherent QPSK systems or gesture-controlling a robot via ESP32, I find the most interesting problems at disciplinary boundaries.

Outside the lab, I lead the Fine Arts department of PDEU's Science & Technical Committee — because good engineering, like good art, demands both rigor and imagination.
